AI Career Navigator

An AI career recommendation tool that analyzes resumes and suggests personalized learning paths using NLP and the Groq API.

Project Summary

AI Career Navigator analyzes resumes and skill data to recommend learning paths and career options with improved relevance over keyword-only systems.

Key Benefits

  • NLP-based resume analysis for stronger candidate matching.
  • Personalized suggestions for upskilling and career growth.
  • Integrated Groq-powered LLM inference to enhance result quality and speed.
  • Fast, low-latency AI responses for a smoother user experience.
